# Tanya M. Lawson

This repository contains the source code for **TanyaMLawson.com** —
the personal and professional website of Tanya M. Lawson.

## About Tanya

Tanya M. Lawson is the founder of CREATORSEAL CORPORATION and the creator of
CREATORSEAL, The Memory Firewall, The Human Texture Writing Engine, Dual Lane
Writing Method, The Veto Layer, and OddLaw. Her work centers on **Proof before
Trust**: helping creators, everyday builders, and AI systems preserve evidence,
protect context, and avoid false authority.

She pairs 30+ years of administrative and operational leadership with practical,
guardrailed use of modern AI.

## Purpose

The site is designed to:
- load extremely fast
- communicate trust and clarity
- show who Tanya is, what she builds, and how her projects connect
- support employment, partnership, and collaboration conversations
- remain accessible across devices and browsers

It is intentionally built without heavy frameworks.

## Stack

- Static HTML5 with Jekyll (GitHub Pages)
- Modern CSS (mobile-first, responsive)
- Minimal JavaScript (only where necessary)
- JSON-LD structured data for accurate search and AI indexing

## Design Philosophy

- Performance is a feature
- Clarity over cleverness
- Calm systems over noise
- Proof before trust

## Status

This site is actively maintained and evolves over time.
