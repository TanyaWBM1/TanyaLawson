Data Deletion

One email is all it takes.
How it works
Ask, and it's handled.

This site doesn't store visitor data in any database — there's no account to delete and no tracking profile to erase. The only information I might hold is email you've sent me directly.

If you'd like your messages or contact information deleted from my email records, send a request to support@tanyamlawson.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." I'll confirm once it's done.

What to include
Keep it simple.

• the email address you wrote from
• what you'd like deleted (everything, or something specific)
• that's it
