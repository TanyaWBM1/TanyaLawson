Privacy

Plain language, no fine-print games.
The short version
This site doesn't track you.

This is a static website. It has no analytics scripts, no advertising trackers, no cookies set by me, and no database collecting information about your visit.

If you contact me, your message goes through your own email app to hello@tanyamlawson.com. I use what you send only to respond to you and understand your request. I do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties, and I do not use it for marketing without your consent.

What I receive
Only what you choose to send.

• your name and email, if you write to me
• whatever you include in your message
• nothing else — no visit logs kept by me

Hosting
GitHub Pages serves this site.

Like most web hosts, GitHub may keep standard server logs (such as IP addresses) to run and secure the service. That logging is governed by GitHub's own privacy policies, not by me.

Your choices
You stay in control.

Want something you sent me corrected or deleted? Just ask — see the Data Deletion page. No forms, no hoops.
